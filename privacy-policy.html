6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, update, or delete your personal data. To make a request, email us at support@gems.app.

7. Children's Privacy

Gem$ is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. if you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it promptly.
